Introduction

Welcome to ArtiFlux ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By accessing or using our application, you signify that you have read, understood, and agree to our collection, storage, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Data

We collect minimal personal data to provide cloud synchronization features. This includes:

  • Email Address: Collected when you sign up for an account to enable data syncing across devices.
  • Password: Securely hashed and stored via our authentication provider (Supabase).

2. User Content

We synchronize specific user-content to our secure cloud database to provide cross-device functionality:

  • Article Data: Metadata of articles you save (URLs, titles, excerpts, etc.).
  • Reading Progress: Your interactions with articles (favorites, archives, tags).

3. Automatically Collected Information

The app may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to:

  • Device Information: Model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers (for install tracking/debugging).
  • Log Data: Crash reports and performance logs to help us improve the app.

How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide Service: To authenticate you and synchronize your saved articles and preferences across devices.
  • To Improve the App: To understand how users interact with our app and fix bugs.
  • Communication: We may use your email to send important service-related notices (e.g., password resets). We do not send marketing emails without your consent.

Data Storage and Security

We use Supabase, a third-party backend-as-a-service, to securely store your data.

  • Authentication: User authentication is handled securely by Supabase. We do not store your raw password.
  • Security: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

We may employ third-party companies and services for the following reasons:

  • To facilitate our Service (e.g., Supabase for backend services).
  • To assist us in analyzing how our Service is used.

These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected Personal Data from anyone under the age of 13 without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
